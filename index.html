Photographer

Roy Ezuz

Roy Ezuz was born in 1990 and is currently based in Tel Aviv, Israel. His main focus is street and portrait photography with a touch of fine art. His work is not limited to one place - he shoots across continents, focusing on documenting faces, fleeting moments, and details on cultural aspects and human behaviors.
